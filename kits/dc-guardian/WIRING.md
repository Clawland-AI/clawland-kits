## Wiring Notes

1. **DHT22 pull-up is mandatory.** Without the 4.7 kО© resistor the sensor will not respond.
2. **MQ-2 warm-up:** The MQ-2 requires ~60 seconds of power-on before readings stabilize. PicClaw's `startup_delay` handles this automatically.
3. **MQ-2 sensitivity pot:** The blue trim pot on the module sets the DO threshold. Clockwise = more sensitive (lower ppm to trigger). For data centers, set mid-point and tune after observing baseline readings.
4. **Water sensors placement:** Place one sensor under the CRAC unit drain pan and one near the UPS battery bank. Run sensor leads through cable glands in the enclosure lid.
5. **5V vs 3.3V:** DHT22 works on 3.3V (preferred вҖ” GPIO-safe). MQ-2 heater requires 5V. Never connect MQ-2 DO directly to GPIO without checking вҖ” the DO pin on most modules is 3.3V-safe (open-collector pulled to Vcc through 10 kО©; verify with multimeter before connecting).
6. **Enclosure:** All sensor cables exit through cable glands. The LicheeRV-Nano and breadboard mount inside with M2.5 screws or double-sided foam tape.
